Technical Letter #11
KICK COMBINATIONS

NOTE:
End in tiger stances.
(R) = right; (L) = left.

Kick Combination One
(R) Tiger:
(R) leg steps forward,
(L) cross behind,
(R) side thrust kick,
(R) tiger,
spinning (L) back kick,
(L) tiger,
(R) roundhouse to the head.

Kick Combination Two
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up,
(L) roundhouse to groin,
(L) tiger.
(R) roundhouse,
(R) tiger,
(L) cross behind,
(R) back kick.

Kick Combination Three
(R) Tiger:
(R) leg steps,
(L) cross behind,
(R) side thrust kick, coming down to
(R) tiger,
(L) cross behind,
(R) hooking heel/tow kick (leg doesn't come down), then
(R) roundhouse double hyperextension to head/chest.

Kick Combination Four
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(L) roundhouse to groin,
(L) tiger.
(R) fake front snap kick,
(R) roundhouse to head,
(R) tiger.
(L) spinning back kick.

Kick Combination Five
(L) Tiger:
(Airborne kicks) (R) front snap kick to groin,
(L) roundhouse kick to cheek/head.

Kick Combination Six
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(L) curl kick,
(L) tiger.
(R) roundhouse,
(R) tiger,
(L) cross behind,
(R) hooking heel/toe kick.

Kick Combination Seven
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(L) curl kick,
(L) tiger.
(R) roundhouse to head,
(R) tiger,
step (L) cross behind,
(R) outward crescent kick.

Kick Combination Eight
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(L) curl kick,
(L) roundhouse kick,
(L) tiger.
(R) roundhouse,
(R) tiger,
(L) spinning hook kick.

Kick Combination Nine
(L) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(L) curl kick,
(L) roundhouse kick to body,
(L) tiger.
(R) roundhouse to head,
(R) tiger,
(L) spinning outward crescent kick.

Kick Combination Ten
(R) Tiger:
Short shuffle up
(L) front snap kick,
jumping (R) front thrust kick, step down to
(R) tiger.
Jumping (R) side thrust kick.

Kick Combination Eleven
(R) Tiger:
Shuffle up
(R) roundhouse, stepping down to
180° jumping spinning right back kick.

Kick Combination Twelve
(R) Tiger:
Same as above, except a jumping spinning outward crescent kick.

Kick Combination Thirteen
High horse, any lead:
Jumping side thrust kick,
shuffle up lead roundhouse to head,
rear jumping spinning roundhouse kick (looking like heel, but held in tight, then roundhouse).

Kick Combination Fourteen
High horse, any lead:
Jumping side thrust kick,
shuffle up,
lead inward crescent kick to rear jumping spinning roundhouse kick.

Love, Sifu

Frederic D. King
Founder
